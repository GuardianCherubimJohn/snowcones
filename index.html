About us

Founded in May of 2018, Matt's Sneaux Kones prides itself in providing customers with the best quality snows cones possible. We are a New Orleans style shaved ice stand using all natural cane sugar syrups (No High Fructose Corn Syrup), as well as rotating Splenda options. In our inaugural year we gained a generous following from our work with the Keller Farmers Market as well as multiple event and corporate clients. The amazing support from our customers has lead us to purchase a food trailer where we can be more mobile and provide our services in many areas for the communities we live and work within.
